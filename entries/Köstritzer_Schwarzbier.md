**Köstritzer Schwarzbier** is a yeast cloudy black beer from the Köstritzer brewery. The Köstritzer brewery was founded in 1543 in Bad Köstritz, Thuringia.

- Alcohol: 4,8%.
- Ingredients: water, barley malt, hops, hop extract.
